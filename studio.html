Long story short.

An old English proverb says 'early to bet and early
to rise make a man healthy, wealthy and wise', but
what do we do once we're awake...During the
Christmas season, we only have one word for you:
Garibaldi. This cocktail only has two ingredients
and is perfect at any time be it brunch or cocktail
hour.

Steps

01SQUEEZE THE JUICE OF TWO ORANGES AND POUR THE JUICE INTO A BLENDER

02MIX UNTIL AN AERIAL TEXTURE IS OBTAINED.

03POUR THE CAMPARI INTO A GLASS FILLED WITH ICE.

04TOP WITH AN ORANGE QUARTER.
